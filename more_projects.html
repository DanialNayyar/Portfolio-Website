Simulation & Structural Analysis of Curved Pipe Flow


This project explored fluid behaviour in curved geometries, it combined the theoretical research & computational simulations by using SolidWorks to create the pipe geometry and ANSYS software to model laminar and turbulent flow in a straight & 180° bend (U-shaped) pipe. Mesh density effects, and the pipe's structural integrity under pressure surges were investigated. The formation of Dean vorticies was also examined and compared against hand calculations and literature data.

Tools: ANSYS, MATLAB, Python, SolidWorks
