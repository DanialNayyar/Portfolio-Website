Lifting System: Six synchronized hydraulic jacks lift the bridge to engage the gear system. Designed to lift 27,968 kN, evenly split (4,661 kN per jack), with relief valves and ISO 46 oil to prevent overloads.

Rotational System: Gear-driven rotation powered by a motor operating at 750 RPM. The bridge rotates 90¬∞ in 5 minutes using a trapezoidal motion profile, with peak torque requirements of 277.95 kNm and motor power ~18.2 kW.

Locking System: A dovetail-style locking mechanism aligns and secures the bridge deck at rest. Spring damping prevents impact stress during closing. Brake clamps and sensors ensure emergency stop capability.
